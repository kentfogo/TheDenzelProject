THE Denzel Washington Page

Quotes

The world was not ready...

Born in 1954 to a preacher who held down three jobs and a no nonsense mother, Denzel was understandably a bit lost when he left home as an adult. After a woeful semester at Fordham University, a 1.8 gpa! , and deciding not to join the army, he decided to take acting seriously. In a few months’ time, he rented a roach-infested apartment and the world was about to be introduced to a force of nature…

The ascent begins...

While Denzel's first lead role was as Roger Porter in Carbon Copy, he really made and impression on the hospital drama, St. Elsewhere. Audiences couldn't get enough of him on the six-season run, he was simply electifiying.

Superstar!

Two years after leaving St. Elsewhere, Denzel was a bonafider superstar. As the lead in movies such as Malcolm X, Mo' Better Blues, Glory and Philadelphia, he was minted as on of the best actors ever.

Passing it forward

Now in his 60's, Denzel is part of the old guard of Hollywood. He has inspired many actors with his incredible performance and has funded scholarships for black actors including the late, great Chadwick Boseman. As an "acting" paternal figure, Denzel has fathered many sons such as Will Smith, Jamie Fox and so on...

Pop Debates

Who is Better? Denzel Washington vs. Will Smith

Many detractors of Denzel and his acting prowess like to bring up Will Smith as a comparison. As if he is worthy of this matchup. Points for Denzel are as follows:

  • One of the best dramatic actors of his time
  • Better Historical/Historical Fiction Movies such as: Malcolm X, Remember the Titans, Glory,
  • Awards: Denzel has won Oscars and Golden Globes. Will Smith has not.
  • The bad guy: Denzel simply isn't afraid to play the bad guy. The anti-hero. The corrupt cop. The drug lord. Will Smith is always the guy saving the day.

What does Will have over Denzel?

  • Will Smith has more comedic roles than Denzel. He's one of the best at making you life and just bringing a...pleasant tone to the screen.
  • Fresh Prince of Bel-Air: A culture defining series. Better than anyhting Denzel has done on television.
  • Animated Movies: Will Smith flourished in animated movies. See A Shark's Tale.

The better actor is..Denzel!
